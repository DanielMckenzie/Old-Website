Research Interests.

I am working with my advisor Mitchell Rothstein on a higher dimensional analog of a construction, due to Krichever, which associates to an algebraic curve with a distinguished line bundle a solution to the KdV equation. Specifically, we are studying a way to associate to the data of a ruled surface over a hyperelliptic curve togehter with a degree zero line bundle a solution to the Schwarzian KdV equation.

I am also involved a VRG group led by Angela Gibney investigating conformal blocks vector bundles on the moduli space of genus zero curves with n marked points.
