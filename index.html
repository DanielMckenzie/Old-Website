About me.

I am a rising fifth year graduate student at the University of Georgia . On the right is a photo of me and my dad; I am the one on the left.

On the 6th of June 2016 I passed my oral exam and was admitted to candidacy.

Before coming to Georgia I was an undergraduate at University of Cape Town from where I also received my Master's degree.

In between Cape Town and Athens, Georgia I spent a semester at the University of Bayreuth in Germany, working with Professor Fabrizio Catanese and his Lehrstuhl.

You can find a copy of my professional resume here .

Research Interests.

I am primarily interested in applied and computational mathematics, and my advisor is Dr. Ming-Jun Lai

Currently, we are wrapping up a project that involves applying compressed sensing to clustering problems in Data Science. More details to follow shortly!

I also have a side interest in Mathematical Finance. In `Notes' you can find some presentations and reports that fall loosely in this category.

In a previous life I worked in pure mathematics, specifically algebraic and differential geometry.
